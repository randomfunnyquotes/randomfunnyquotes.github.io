"Throw away that cigarette," I said. To my amazement, he did, promptly. I was beginning to wonder whether I had not been too abrupt, he gave me a curious sensation of being a man of my own age, when he produced a silver case from his pocket and opened it. I saw that the cigarette in the fender was a stump.

- The Little Nugget (P. G. Wodehouse)(quote number 1055)
