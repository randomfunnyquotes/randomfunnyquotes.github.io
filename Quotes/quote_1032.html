Elderly Stranger (to bridegroom). Sir, I have jigged your wife on my knee. The Bridegroom (with absent politeness). Ah! Lately? Elderly Stranger. When she was a baby, sir. The Bridegroom (from force of habit). Oh, thanks. Thanks awf'lly.

- Love Among the Chickens / A Story of the Haps and Mishaps on an English Chicken Farm (P. G. Wodehouse)(quote number 1032)
