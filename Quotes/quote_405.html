"I suppose you are wondering what it's all about?" she said. This was precisely what George was wondering most consumedly. "No, no," he said. "Not at all. It's not my business." "And of course you're much too well bred to be inquisitive about other people's business?" "Of course I am. What was it all about?" "I'm afraid I can't tell you."

- A Damsel in Distress (P. G. Wodehouse)(quote number 405)
