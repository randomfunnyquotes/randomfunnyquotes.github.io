“Oscillation upon the pavement always means an affaire de cœur. She would like advice, but is not sure that the matter is not too delicate for communication. And yet even here we may discriminate. When a woman has been seriously wronged by a man she no longer oscillates, and the usual symptom is a broken bell wire.

- Adventures of Sherlock Holmes / Illustrated (Arthur Conan Doyle)(quote number 658)
