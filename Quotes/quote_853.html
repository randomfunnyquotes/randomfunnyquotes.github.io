"Some pip!" observed Mr Trevis. Otis Pilkington awoke from day-dreams with a start. "What did you say?" "That girl … I said she was some pippin!" "Miss Mariner," said Mr Pilkington icily, "is a most charming, refined, cultured, and vivacious girl, if you mean that." "Yes," said Mr Trevis. "That was what I meant!"

- The Little Warrior (Wodehouse, P. G. (Pelham Grenville))(quote number 853)
