"You remember Dora Mason?"

"Who is Dora Mason?" enquired Millie, quickly.

"A plain, elderly sort of female who used to be my aunt's secretary," replied Ukridge, with equal promptness.

Personaly, I remembered Miss Mason as a rather unusually pretty and attractive girl, but I felt that it would be injudicious to say so.

- Ukridge (P. G. Wodehouse)(quote number 1023)
