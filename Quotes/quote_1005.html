How the small boys will laugh when they see a man in a helmet go by in a car! I shan't notice them myself because it's a little difficult to notice anything from inside this thing, but I'm afraid it will be rather unpleasant for you … I know what we'll do. We'll go to London and drive up and down Piccadilly! That will be fun!" There was a long silence. "Is my helmet on straight?" said Sam.

- Three Men and a Maid (Wodehouse, P. G. (Pelham Grenville))(quote number 1005)
